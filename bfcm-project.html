PumPums™ MEGA SALE – Thanksgiving + BFCM Campaign

For this project, I created a full 11-day BFCM (Black Friday–Cyber Monday) Mega Sale campaign for PumPums™, centered on natural relief, high-converting offers, and strategic customer engagement. This included complete email sequences, social media plans, and website copy direction.

Key Contributions:

  • Campaign Direction & Strategy: Developed the theme “Natural Relief Meets Real Savings,” with an 11-day sale structure and a headline offer of up to 40% off.
  • Email Campaign Development: Created a multi-phase email flow (Launch, Reminder, Last-Day) with A/B testing options, social proof, and urgent CTAs to maximize conversions.
  • Brand Voice & Messaging: Ensured all copy used warm, empowering, women-first messaging that balanced scientific appeal with emotional connection.
