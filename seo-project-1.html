Project Scope

Landing page designed to drive email signups, educate users on gut health, and improve organic visibility for gut-health keywords.

Role

SEO Content Creator & Landing Page Optimizer

Project Goal

  • Drive email signups for the free Gentle Gut Reset Guide.
  • Educate users on gut health and benefits of a gentle gut reset.
  • Capture leads tagged with “gutreset” via Mailchimp.
  • Improve organic search visibility for keywords like gut cleanse and mango cleanse.

Actions Taken

Keyword & Content Strategy:

  • Targeted primary keywords: gut cleanse, gentle gut reset, reduce bloating, support digestion.
  • Integrated long-tail keywords naturally into headlines, bullet points, and body copy.

Optimized meta title & description:

  • Meta Title: Free Gentle Gut Reset eBook | Zenita Naturals
  • Meta Description: Download Zenita Naturals' free gut cleanse guide to reduce bloating, support digestion, and feel better naturally, no fasting required.

On-Page SEO:

  • H1/H2 headings and bullet points optimized with target keywords.
  • Images optimized with descriptive alt text (flipbook mockup, ingredient visuals).
  • Internal linking to related blog posts and products where relevant.

Technical SEO & UX:

  • Mobile-friendly, fast-loading design.
  • CLS-friendly image layout, lazy-loading images, WebP/AVIF formats.
  • Mailchimp integration for automated lead capture and Google Analytics tracking.

Conversion Optimization:

  • Clear, keyword-rich CTA buttons: “Get My Free Gut Reset Guide.”
  • Lead capture form optimized for usability and automated tagging (gutreset).
  • Visual mockups of eBook and ingredients to reinforce value.

Results & Improvements

Overall SEO Performance:

  • Total Clicks: 615
  • Total Impressions: 6.59K
  • Average CTR: 9.3%
  • Average Position: 25.3

Top Keywords Driving Traffic:

  • zenita naturals
  • 15 day mango cleanse
  • zenita mango cleanse
  • mango cleanse

Improvements Achieved:

  • Organic Traffic Growth: Landing page attracted targeted clicks for relevant keywords.
  • Keyword Visibility: Ranked for both primary and long-tail keywords aligned with search intent.
  • Lead Capture: Optimized content and CTAs increased email signups.
  • Content Effectiveness: Clear messaging, visual elements, and structure improved user engagement and conversion.

Skills Demonstrated

  • SEO Content Strategy & Keyword Optimization
  • On-Page SEO & Conversion-Focused Copywriting
  • Technical SEO Awareness (mobile, CLS, images)
  • Analytics & Data-Driven Insights
  • CMS & Email Automation Integration (Mailchimp)
