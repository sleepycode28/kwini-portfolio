12-Day Email Campaign for PumPums

For this project, I developed a complete 12-day promotional email campaign for PumPums, focused on driving sales, customer engagement, and product reviews during a seasonal marketing push. The campaign was strategically designed to position PumPums as a go-to comfort and wellness essential.

Key Contributions:

  • Campaign Strategy & Concept Development: Created the overarching concept: “12 Days of Comfort — New Deal Every Day.” Developed a structured, day-by-day promo plan including BOGO deals, percentage discounts, and engagement-driven giveaways.
  • Email Marketing Execution: Wrote 12 complete daily email copies, ensuring each aligned with the daily promo offer and the overall comfort-focused brand message.
  • Social Media Copywriting: Created daily social media captions for all 12 days, matching the fun, relatable tone of the campaign.
