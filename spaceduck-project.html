UX & Feature Writing: Spaceduck

Project Overview

This project focused on improving the user experience of the Spaceduck website by crafting clear, engaging, and intuitive copy. The primary goal was to communicate product features and benefits effectively, enhance the brand voice, and drive user engagement through actionable, feature-focused writing.

UX Writing / Microcopy:

  • Homepage & Landing Pages: Wrote concise headlines, subheadings, and body copy to clearly communicate value and guide users.
  • CTA & Button Text: Developed actionable microcopy like “Start Your Journey,” “Explore Missions,” “Join the Crew.”
  • Navigation & Form Copy: Optimized menus, section headings, tooltips, and form instructions for clarity and accessibility.
  • Error Messages & Notifications: Created friendly, helpful copy for errors, confirmations, and alerts, maintaining a cohesive brand tone.

Feature Writing / Content:

  • Feature Sections & Product Pages: Crafted copy to highlight Spaceduck’s key features, benefits, and unique offerings.
  • Storytelling: Used approachable, adventurous language to communicate how features support user goals.
  • Visual Integration: Paired feature descriptions with graphics, screenshots, or icons to improve comprehension.
  • Consistency: Maintained brand voice across all features, emphasizing fun, exploratory, and engaging experiences.

Results & Impact

  • Enhanced UX Flow: Users could navigate intuitively, understanding both site structure and feature offerings.
  • Increased Engagement: Actionable CTAs and clear feature copy guided users to explore and interact with key elements.
  • Stronger Brand Voice: Copy reflected Spaceduck’s playful, adventurous identity, making features approachable and compelling.
  • Content Foundation: Established clear UX writing standards and feature storytelling frameworks for future updates.

A Note on Spaceduck

The company has announced it is winding down, with a blog post explaining the closure. Their LinkedIn profile also provides updates and company history.
