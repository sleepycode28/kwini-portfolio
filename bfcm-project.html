PumPums™ MEGA SALE – Thanksgiving + BFCM Campaign

For this project, I created a full 11-day BFCM (Black Friday–Cyber Monday) Mega Sale campaign for PumPums™, centered on natural relief, high-converting offers, and strategic customer engagement. This included complete email sequences, social media plans, and website copy direction.

Campaign Direction & Strategy:

  • Developed the overall theme: “PumPums™ MEGA SALE: Natural Relief Meets Real Savings.”
  • Positioned the sale around Thanksgiving + BFCM, running from Nov 22 – Dec 2, 2025.
  • Crafted the main offer: Up to 40% Off Sitewide + Free Shipping on $50+.
  • Defined the primary tagline: “Your Period. Your Peace. Your Best Deal Yet.”

Email Campaign Development (Launch, Reminder & Last-Day Phases):

I created a complete email flow with 3 distinct phases:

1. Launch Phase Emails:

  • Wrote multiple strong, high-converting subject lines, preview texts, headers, subheaders, and CTAs.
  • Developed three full body copy options for A/B testing or segmentation.
  • Highlighted: Menstrual Relief Gummies, ACV Gummies, Hydration + Relief Bundles
  • Added a brand education section: “Why Women Choose PumPums™” with plant-based and Pickle+ benefits.
  • Included a CTA to learn about the product science.

2. Reminder Phase Emails:

  • Created friendly, persuasive reminders promoting: Ongoing 40% off, Free shipping on $50+, Best-selling natural relief items
  • Added social proof lines, reviews, and a callout for the PumPums Perks Club with double points.

3. Last-Day Phase Emails:

  • Built urgent, conversion-focused messaging for final hours of the sale.
  • Provided multiple body copy variations emphasizing: Last chance, Midnight cutoff, 1,000+ women already stocked up
  • Included testimonials and Perks Club bonus reminders.
